🔒 Privacy & Ethics

What We Collect

  • ✅ Search queries on Google (if enabled)
  • ✅ AI Overview presence and content
  • ✅ Cited source URLs and domains
  • ✅ Click interactions with citations
  • ✅ Timestamps and session IDs

What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Personal identifying information
  • ❌ Passwords or form data
  • ❌ Browsing history outside Google Search
  • ❌ Email addresses or account details
  • ❌ Any data from other websites

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data is sent to external servers. You have full control over your data and can export or delete it at any time.

ℹ️ About

AI Overview Tracker is a research tool designed to understand how users interact with Google's AI-generated search results. This extension helps researchers analyze the impact of AI Overviews on search behavior and information consumption patterns.
